# Level 1: Absolute Beginner (CEFR A0–A1)
- Focus: Foundations and basic survival English.
- Vocabulary: Letters (A–Z), numbers (1–100), colors, days of the week, months, and basic greetings.
- Daily Life: Family members, common food and drinks, household objects, body parts, and simple feelings/emotions.
- Grammar: Verb "to be" (present/past), singular/plural nouns, personal pronouns, and definite/indefinite articles (a, an, the).
- Key Phrases: "My name is...", "How much is this?", and "Where is the...?".

# Level 2: Elementary (CEFR A2)
- Focus: Simple sentences and frequently used expressions.
- Topics: Shopping, local geography, employment/jobs, and routine leisure activities.
- Grammar: Past Simple (regular and irregular verbs), Present Continuous (now), and basic modal verbs (can, could, should, must).
- Comparisons: Using comparatives and superlatives (bigger, the biggest).
Time: Telling time precisely and using prepositions of time/place (in, on, at).

# Level 3: Intermediate (CEFR B1–B2)
- Focus: Independent communication and expressing opinions.
- Topics: Travel experiences, entertainment (movies/music), health and fitness, and environmental issues.
- Grammar: Future tenses (will, going to), Present Perfect vs. Past Simple, and the Passive Voice.
- Logic: 1st and 2nd Conditionals for hypothetical situations (If I were you...).
- Vocabulary: Phrasal verbs, common idioms, and connectors for cause and effect (because, so, therefore).

# Level 4: Advanced (CEFR C1–C2)
- Focus: Fluency, nuance, and professional/academic use.
- Topics: Business ethics, technology’s impact on society, complex news events, and abstract concepts like philosophy or art.
- Grammar: Mixed conditionals, 3rd conditional (regrets), advanced inversion for style, and complex sentence structures.
- Refinement: Subtle jokes, regional accents/dialects, and industry-specific vocabulary.
-Professional: Writing formal reports, resumes, and academic essays.

# Feature in this App:
- Gamification: Use "streaks," rewards, and bite-sized lessons to keep users motivated.
- For Kids: Include animated songs, fairy tales, and character-based stories.
- For Adults: Add practical sections for "Business Magazine" style reading or email writing.
- Multi-Modal: Combine reading, writing, listening, and AI-powered speaking practice.
